Music Player

A simple and small music player side project for designing lessons.

This is by no means a big or complicated design, it is simple, direct and modern. The player comes to life with techno-style animations whenever the music is playing, but when the music stops the application just dies, the animations will simply stop playing. It is quite a simple concept that shows that techno music is alive, powered with the souls of every music lover, but it is very effective.
